Achievements

Since it’s inception, Telangana Open School Society has been very successful in enrolling and certifying thousands of learners. Over the past four academic years alone, over 3,40,000 learners have enrolled in TOSS’s SSC and Intermediate schemes, out of whom, over 1,40,000 learners have been certified across both the schemes. Below is a break-up of the figures for the past four academic years.
