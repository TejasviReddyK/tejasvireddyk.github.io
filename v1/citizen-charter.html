Citizen Charter

Duplicate Memo of Marks - SSC

The following documents are required:

  • Filled in application form through AI Coordinator
  • Prescribed fee through DD

Fee: Rs.100/-

Time frame: 07 working days

Officer to be contacted for delay or default in service: The Joint Director

Duplicate Memo of Marks - Intermediate

The following documents are required:

  • Filled in application form through AI Coordinator
  • Prescribed fee through DD

Fee: Rs.200/-

Time frame: 07 working days

Officer to be contacted for delay or default in service: The Joint Director

Duplicate Pass Certificate - SSC

The following documents are required:

  • Filled in application form forwarded through the AI Coordinator with photo attestation
  • Xerox copy of pass certificate
  • Police enquiry certificate (non availability)
  • Affidavit (Rs.50/-) signed by Notary/1st Class Magistrate
  • DD for Rs.250/-

Fee: Rs.250/-

Time frame: 10 working days

Officer to be contacted for delay or default in service: The Joint Director

Correction in Pass certificate - SSC

  • Covering letter through the AI Coordinator

  • Original TC cum Migration certificate
  • Original SSC Pass certificate
  • Xerox copy of admission application form attested by AI Coordinator
  • In case of Mothers name correction Ration Card/ Birth certificate
  • Original TC/ Record sheet

Fee: -

Time frame: 07 working days

Officer to be contacted for delay or default in service: The Joint Director
