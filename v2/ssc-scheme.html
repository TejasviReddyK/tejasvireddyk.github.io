SSC Scheme of Studies

The Scheme of Studies for Secondary Course is shown in Figure 1 below this section. To obtain the SSC pass certificate, the candidate is required to pass in a minimum of five subjects including one or a maximum of two languages from Group A and a minimum two subjects from Group B. The candidate can opt for one vocational subject, from Group C, totaling the number of subjects to five. If the candidate does not wish to opt for a vocational subject from Group C, they can take a fifth subject either from Group A (limited to 2 subjects) or from Group B (3 or 4 subjects) totaling to five subjects. However, the candidate is free to take one additional subject (sixth). Thus, in all the candidate can choose a maximum of six subjects. Please go through carefully Section1.1 above in respect to choice of subjects.
