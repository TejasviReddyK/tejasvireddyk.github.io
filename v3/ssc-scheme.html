To obtain the SSC pass certificate:

  • The candidate is required to pass in a minimum of five subjects including one or a maximum of two languages from Group A and a minimum two subjects from Group B. The candidate can opt for one vocational subject, from Group C, totaling the number of subjects to five.
  • If the candidate does not wish to opt for a vocational subject from Group C, they can take a fifth subject either from Group A (limited to 2 subjects) or from Group B (3 or 4 subjects) totaling to five subjects. However, the candidate is free to take one additional subject (sixth).

Thus, in all the candidate can choose a maximum of six subjects.

To simplify further, the choice of subjects can be done as follows.

  1. For 5 subjects, the combinations can be

    No. of subjects from Group A No. of subjects from Group B No. of subjects from Group C Total no. of subjects
    Combination 1 1 4 0 1 + 4 + 0 = 5
    Combination 2 2 3 0 2 + 3 + 0 = 5
    Combination 3 2 2 1 2 + 2 + 1 = 5
    Combination 4 1 3 1 1 + 3 + 1 = 5

  2. For 6 subjects, the combinations can be

    No. of subjects from Group A No. of subjects from Group B No. of subjects from Group C Total no. of subjects
    Combination 1 1 5 0 1 + 5 + 0 = 6
    Combination 2 1 4 1 1 + 4 + 1 = 6
    Combination 3 2 4 0 2 + 4 + 0 = 6
    Combination 4 2 3 1 2 + 3 + 1 = 6
    Combination 5 3 3 0 3 + 3 + 0 = 6
    Combination 6 3 2 1 3 + 2 + 1 = 6
